About Us

COMPETENCE

The chef must be exceptional at what he or she does: a great cook, a balanced manager, an inspiring leader, and a consummate communicator. Which is what characterizes our chefs!

EXPERIENCE

By the time a person reaches the level of chef, he or she should have matured as a competent professional, having made most of the junior mistakes that are expected of cooks and sous chefs.Our chefs have plenty of it!

PALATE

In the end, the food is all about flavour. The chefs have a well-developed, vastly experienced palate and the ability to build flavour profiles that will set our restaurant apart.

KNOWLEDGE

Our chefs have an encyclopedic knowledge of food, the ingredients available, the seasonality of those ingredients, the methods of cooking most appropriate, and how to compensate the flaws in any of the ingredients.

TEAM FOCUS

In an ideal situation, the well-trained team eventually becomes part of the menu design process, networks with vendors and growers so that a connection is made with the source, and learns to work together to reach common goals.

RESPECT

This is a multifaceted attribute that requires the chef to respect ingredients, staff, established methods of cooking, the customer and the business itself.
